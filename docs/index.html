The Rolling Scopes
Conference

Minsk, 9-11 August 2019

Buy Tickets Call for Speakers

The biggest conference in Belarus focusing on developing and designing Web, JavaScript, Node.JS, Mobile, Smart TV, VR/AR applications. Backed by The Rolling Scopes developer community since 2015.

This year The Rolling Scopes Conference will go beyond Front-end and JavaScript and utilize full three days of discussions about JavaScript, Front-end, Frameworks, Mobile, Smart TV, Node.JS.
